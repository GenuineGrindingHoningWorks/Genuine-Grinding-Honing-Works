OUR BRANDS

GENUINE REMAN

Genuine Reman™ is a global hydraulics remanufacturing brand offering extensive maintenance solutions for nearly all OE hydraulic products. Our large inventory ensures swift component replacement, backed by unmatched in-house technical expertise and OEM-compliant testing and service.

GENUINE SERVICE

Our service division focuses on hydraulic repairs and rebuilds. With 40+ years of experience and advanced facilities, we serve clients across various industries. We ensure high-quality repairs with minimal turnaround, affordable pricing, and even FREE evaluations.
