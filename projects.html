Markets We Serve

We provide precision-engineered hydraulic cylinders for a wide range of demanding industries, ensuring durability, reliability, and peak performance.

Aerospace

Custom welded body hydraulic rod and telescopic cylinders help aircraft landing gear, jetways, ground & baggage handling equipment, and more function optimally.

Construction Equipment

Quality cylinders with the durability to withstand extreme pressure, temperature, shock, vibration, and contamination for construction OEMs.

Off-Road Equipment

We have the technical knowledge to help off-road equipment deliver top performance under the harshest conditions, meeting or exceeding industry requirements.

Automotive

Our aluminum double-acting and telescopic hydraulic cylinders feature unique designs that have set the standard for reliability in auto carrier and truck equipment applications.

Marine

We offer high degrees of reliability and corrosion resistance for applications ranging from personal watercraft boat lifts to 10,000 lb. davits on cruise ships.

Agriculture

Our hydraulic cylinders help farm equipment OEMs offer the durability and low cost of ownership required to achieve long operating life and meet production goals.

Our Applications

From heavy-duty construction to automotive transport, our cylinders are designed for performance and longevity in a variety of critical applications.

Truck Lift Gates

We design and manufacture custom hydraulic cylinders for truck lift gates. Rugged, reliable, and with compact designs, our lift gate cylinders are ideal for everything from pickup trucks to box trucks and semis.

Road Pavement & Planers

Our products are used by the leading manufacturers of asphalt paving equipment in the USA. They are built to survive the rugged construction environment extremely well.

Turf Care & Handlers

Our ability to manufacture high-quality hydraulic cylinders designed for the temperature ranges and high vibration requirements of the turf industry, at a competitive cost, has made us a leader in this market.

Utility Trucks

We manufacture over 150,000 hydraulic cylinders per year for dump bodies, lift gates, and utility cranes. We have custom-designed thousands of rams and cylinders to meet the requirements of these applications.

Concrete Pumps

Our cylinders survive the rugged construction environment of concrete pumping and have been specified on many of the pumping units built in the USA.

Snowplows

We are a leading supplier of hydraulic cylinders for the snow removal industry. Our cylinders are designed to accommodate this difficult, corrosive and high-contaminant environment.

Mobile Cranes

We have unsurpassed experience designing custom hydraulic cylinders for every application in the mobile crane industry, giving safety factors the foremost consideration to prevent issues like thermal drifting or rod buckling.

Auto Transport

Our unique cylinder designs have made us the leading supplier in the auto carrier industry. Our aluminum double-acting and telescopic cylinders have set the standard, utilizing premium coatings to provide longer life and reduce maintenance.
